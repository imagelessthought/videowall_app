Currently the individual video wall displays are configured for 1920 x 1080 resolution (16:9 aspect ratio; 1080p). The overall resolution of the videowall is (3840 x 2160).

  • All elements are equally sized: one element.
  • Aspect ratio required for images/video: 16:9.
  • Best display performance is achieved with 3840 x 2160.

The larger the file, the slower the load time for both images and videos. If possible, we recommend the webp format for images, and webm for videos. But other formats that are also optimized for online deliverey are out there.
